SUPPLEMENTAL MOTION FOR APPROPRIATE RELIEF

Last Revised • June 30, 2026

Official Claim Title

LAW ENFORCEMENT MISCONDUCT CAUSED THE DEFENDANT TO BE DENIED A FAIR TRIAL, DUE PROCESS, AND HIS RIGHT TO BE PROTECTED FROM WITNESSING AGAINST HIMSELF, PROTECTED BY THE FIFTH, SIXTH, AND FOURTEENTH AMENDMENTS OF THE UNITED STATES CONSTITUTION AND ARTICLE I, SECTIONS 19, 23, 24, 27, AND 36 OF THE NORTH CAROLINA CONSTITUTION.

Section Overview

The Introduction provides an overview of the Supplemental Motion and summarizes the newly discovered information upon which the motion is based. It introduces the Defendant's allegations regarding four public agencies, explains how those allegations relate to the Defendant's convictions, and outlines the constitutional basis for the relief requested.
